Anti-Piracy and Content Protection

We are dedicated to enforcing intellectual property laws and holding those who engage in piracy accountable for their actions. We work with law enforcement agencies to investigate and prosecute piracy cases, and we support civil litigation to recover damages and deter future piracy.

We aim to prevent piracy and enforce intellectual property laws to ensure a fair and just marketplace.

If you are a content creator, publisher, or distributor, we provides resources and assistance to aid you in protecting your property and taking appropriate measures against piracy. We also advocate for consumers to support the creative industries by legally consuming licensed content.
